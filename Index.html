NUMBERBLOCK ONE

One, or 1, is the small red Numberblock made up of 1 block. She is the main protagonist. One first appears in the episode with the same name. She is voiced by Beth Chalmers.

NUMBERBLOCK TWO

Two, or 2, is a Numberblock made up of 2 orange blocks. He has glasses, socks, and shoes. He first appears in Another One. Two and all the other even numbers have flat tops when they stand 2 blocks wide, as demonstrated in Odds and Evens. He is very kind and is also One's best friend.

NUMBERBLOCK THREE

Three, or 3, is the Numberblock made up of 3 blocks. She first appears in the episode with the same name. She is voiced by Beth Chalmers, who also voices One, Five, Eleven, Sixteen, Twenty-Four, Thirty, Fifty, and Blockzilla.

NUMBERBLOCK Four

Four, or 4, is the Numberblock made up of 4 lime green blocks. His favorite shape to be is a square.
